Who is Daniel Turanov?

I’m a performer first — an actor and musician building a life around stage, story, and sound. I love roles that really demand honesty and transformation, the kind where you can’t just hide behind the lines. I’m constantly training, sharpening craft, and chasing that moment where a scene (or a song) makes a room go quiet.

On the music side, I perform and write with my younger brother which is a duo project shaped by disco-funk energy, modern pop/R&B polish, and a lot of live-show ambition. We’re focused on truly doing fantastic work and we want to shock the world with our music and performance.

Offstage, I’m really obsessive about growth: creating, practicing, refining. I’m building a professional presence, pushing my craft through new projects, and staying ready for bigger rooms, bigger sets, and bigger stages.
