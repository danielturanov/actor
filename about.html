Who is Daniel Turanov?


I’m an actor. I’m a singer. I’m a performer.

I’ve always felt like I was meant to build a life around stories, music, and moments that make people feel something. Acting, to me, is not just about saying lines. It’s about becoming honest enough to let people see something real.

I love roles that ask something from me — roles that force me to transform, listen, take risks, and step into another person’s world. That’s the kind of work I’m chasing.

Music is the other side of it. I perform and write with my younger brother, and together we’re building something that feels alive — a mix of disco, funk, pop, R&B, soul, and the kind of energy that belongs on a real stage. We don’t just want to make songs. We want to create moments.

I’m obsessive about getting better. I practice, study, create, fail, adjust, and keep going. I’m not interested in being average. I want to grow into the kind of artist who can walk into a room, step onto a stage, or show up on screen and make people pay attention.

What should you expect to find here?
Work · Process · Momentum

This site is a piece of that journey. Here you’ll find my headshots, reel work, credits, music, and the world I’m building around all of it. It’s not just a portfolio. It’s a snapshot of the work, the process, and the momentum.

If you’re a casting director, filmmaker, creative, musician, or someone who wants to make something that actually matters, I’d love to connect.
