What is Daniel Turanov?

Daniel Turanov is an actor, storyteller, and dreamer. From his first high-school monologue to leading roles in short films like Blood Bound and Silence, he’s driven by a passion for bringing characters to life.

Currently represented by Advent Management and Phirgun Mair, Daniel balances on-set experience with continuous training in the Stanislavski method. Whether in Chicago, New York, or Los Angeles, he pursues each project with unwavering dedication.
